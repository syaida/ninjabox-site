Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

We specialize in four main types of boxes: Pizza Boxes (heat-resistant and grease-proof), Mailer Boxes (perfect for e-commerce shipping), RSC Boxes (Regular Slotted Container for general storage), and Document Boxes (ideal for archiving files and records). All our boxes are available in various sizes and can be customized to meet your specific needs.

Yes! We offer custom printing services for all our box types. You can add your company logo, branding, product information, or any design you prefer. Contact us with your requirements, and our team will provide you with pricing and design options.

Our minimum order quantity varies depending on the type of box and customization requirements. For standard boxes, we offer flexible quantities to suit businesses of all sizes. Please contact us with your specific needs, and we'll provide you with the best solution and competitive pricing.

Delivery time depends on the order quantity, customization requirements, and your location in Malaysia. Standard orders typically take 5-10 business days, while custom printed boxes may take 10-15 business days. We also offer express delivery options for urgent orders. Contact us for specific delivery timeframes.

Yes! We are committed to sustainability. Our boxes are made from recyclable corrugated cardboard materials that are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. We can also provide eco-friendly printing options using soy-based inks upon request.

Absolutely! We deliver to all states across Malaysia. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure your boxes arrive safely and on time, wherever you are located in the country.

Yes, we can provide samples for you to evaluate the quality and suitability of our boxes. Sample charges may apply and will be discussed based on your requirements. Contact us to request samples.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, online banking, and corporate accounts for registered businesses. Payment terms can be discussed based on order value and business relationship. Please contact us for more details on payment options.
